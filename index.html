Jonathan Lehman

United States Air Force Veteran

Here is a little about me, Jonathan.

The beginning…Straight out of high school I went to United States Air Force basic training at Lackland AFB in San Antonio, TX. After basic I went to Tech School for Aerospace Ground Equipment at Shepherd AFB in Wichita Falls, TX. My first, and only, duty station was at Davis-Monthan AFB. I was assigned to the 355 EMS supporting the A-10 airframe and other air sorties that came in on TDY. Nearing the end of my enlistment I sustained injuries to both of my knees which resulted in a medical separation from the Air Force. I loved what I did, but physically my body was unable to keep doing what I loved most.

More About Jonathan, if you care..

The journey continues… After the Air Force I decided to use my post 9/11 GI bill to pursue an education at Oklahoma State University where I majored in Plant and Soil Science. After graduation I move to Northwest Arkansas. While living here, I connected with a few friends that introduced me to Road Cycling. I loved it so much I jumped fully into the sport and haven’t looked back.

Looking to the future… My current job in the transportation business had gotten to a point where I was no longer being challenged and had climbed the latter. I had always had a fascination and desire to learn coding, so I decided to try and pick up what I can off the internet.
